About

TheaterOrChill offers ticketing and rental services for movies showing in theaters. For the same price, you can watch the latest movies at a theater near you, or at the comfort of your home.

Every day it seems liklier that the near future will require us to learn how to live with COVID-19. We at TheaterOrChill understand that movie enthusiasts around the world want to continue watching new movies as they are released, even as inconveniences caused by COVID-19 lockdown measures and safety protocols continue to impact their lives.

That is why we have launched a platform where movie fans can decide whether to rent or go see a movie in theaters - at no extra cost. Movie rentals are valid while the movies are in theaters. In other words, once you have rented a movie, you have it available to you for as long as it is available on our website. You can stream it as many times as you want! Isn't that cool?

Having said that, we are not discouraging going to the movies. That's why those of you who decide to watch a movie in theaters will earn rewards on every visit! After four visits, you will have enough rewards to get yourself a free ticket to your fifth visit, and a $15 voucher that can be applied to food, drinks, and movie memorabilia at a theater near you.

So, thanks for stopping by and hopefully we will see you again!
